How does this comic relate to a sacred text?

The Name of Text: The Bible/Gospels


The relation: Character reflects a person/deity from sacred text


Main Character: Judas

Summary: Judas Judas has just betrayed Jesus and now sits in Hell with the Devil and banished to keep him company. In hell, Judas sees the suffering and is tormented by the Devil saying it's all Jesus's fault Judas is in Hell. Then something goes wrong, Jesus arrives in Hell.


Entry: Cover: Images that portray the sacrifice of Jesus with thorns that were involved in the crucifixion. Scripture within comic: Matthew 26:24, Ezekiel 1:4-8, Isaiah 53:3-4, Revelation 12:7, Isaiah 14:12, Revelation 4:6-7, John 15:13 What is mentioned in this comic: -God testing Abraham with killing his son -Stories of Pharaoh, David and Goliath, Jezebel, and Lot's wife -The Salvation of the thief on the cross next to Jesus -Jesus walking on water -Jesus beating death In this comic, the illustrations also represent parts of the holy text. There are many instances where the illustrations are not mentioned within the text of the comic storyline. Here are some examples: -Judas's death -Last Supper -Crucifixion -Eve picking the forbidden fruit -Jesus bearing the sin of all the world
